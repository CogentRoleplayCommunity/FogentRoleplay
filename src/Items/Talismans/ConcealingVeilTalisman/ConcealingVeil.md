# Concealing Veil

![Copyrighted Image](ConcealingVeil.png)

## Desc

Talisman put together from dark cloth, with a lustrous sheen.

Conceals the wearer's presence, becoming slightly transparent at will.

A common talisman of assassins.

## Info

Item Type: Talisman

Tier: Flawless (+6)

## Effects

### Enhanced Stealth

+1 to Stealth Skill.
