## Backstory

Jaxon has a dark sense of humor and an even darker past. He’s worked on ships that never came back from their missions, often salvaging what he could from derelict vessels. This earned him the nickname "Graveyard." He’s grim, but reliable, treating every job like it could be his last—and it often feels like it might be. His quarters are decorated with mementos from wrecked ships, a habit that freaks out some of the crew. Jaxon’s experience with disassembling dead vessels makes him the go-to guy for tricky repairs, but his morbid outlook tends to make his team nervous when things start going wrong.
