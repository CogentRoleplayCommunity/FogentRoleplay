## Backstory

Cultists swear allegiance to dark powers. They conceal
their activities to avoid being ostracized, imprisoned, or executed for their beliefs.

## Disposition

- Malleable
- Fanatic
- Irrational

## Goals/Aspirations

- To see the world changed with their cult's values.
